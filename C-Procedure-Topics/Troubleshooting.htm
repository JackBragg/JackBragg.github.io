Troubleshooting Guide

Issues may arise while installing and configuring the various software platforms due to hardware differences or driver incompatibilities. Use this guide to help in alleviate the most common issues.

The system will not recognize my device

Part I:

Poor connections are the primary culprit for this common issue.

  1. Disconnect the VR headset from the system and look inside the connector to ensure it is free of debris.
  2. Check the cable for twists, bends, or fraying. Replace if possible, or refer to your manufacturers warrenty.

Part II:

If connecting to a port other than the graphics card or motherboard, It is most likely that the fresco settings were not configured during set up. Please refer to this section of the guide to configure the settings appropriately.

The VR headset is stuttering or jumping

The Default Super Sampling in XYZ is most commonly set too high for the system hardware used. Please open XYZ and set the Default Super Sampling value to zero and restart your system. If the problem persists please contact your PLEXSYS representative.
