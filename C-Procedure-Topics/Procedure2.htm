Installing SteamVR

With Oculus Home installed and configured, you are now ready to move on to configuring SteamVR enabling access to our most effective training experiences.

*If you have not yet installed and configured Oculus Home, please walk through our guide on Oculus Home procedure.

Steam has streamlined the installation process for both Steam client and SteamVR, allowing you to download both programs from the same page. A Steam account is required for installation.

  1. Navigate to the SteamVR webpage.
  2. Log in to the steam website by clicking login at the top right of the page.
  3. Select Install Steam at the top right of the page.
  4. Follow prompts from Steam installer.
  5. log in to the Steam client.
  6. Navigate back to the SteamVR webpage and select the Install SteamVR option located below the scrolling images.(or you can follow the guide below to install SteamVR from within the Steam client)
  7. When complete, navigate to the Steam client library to ensure SteamVR is present.
  8. Close all programs and restart your computer.

If SteamVR is not showing in the Steam client library, please follow the supplemental guide below

Configuring SteamVR

With Oculus Home installed and configured and SteamVR installed as well, we are ready to configure SteamVR with your VR headset.

  1. If not open already, open and log into Oculus Home.(It must be running in the background)
  2. Start SteamVR from the Steam client.
  3. Select the green box labeled VR in the upper right corner of the Steam client.
  4. A window will appear with green icons indicating your VR devices are recognized. (if the icons are not showing please refer to the troubleshooting section)
  5. Right-click on the window and select Run Room Setup
  6. It is essential to follow the prompts from the Steam client to configure the best operating area for your situation.

Congratulations, you have successfully installed SteamVR and Steam client! Only one step remains until you are able to open the door to enhanced mission planning abilities!

Supplemental Guide

Follow these steps to install SteamVR from within the Steam client rather than the steam website.

  1. Select Library at the top of the client
  2. Under the search bar in the upper left, select Tools in the drop down menu.
  3. In the search bar, type SteamVR and select "SteamVR" from the list under Tools.
  4. In the window, click Install and follow the prompts.

When installation is finished, SteamVR should now show in the Steam client library.
