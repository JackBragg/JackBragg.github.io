Configuring Oculus Home

XYZ is designed to connect Steam VR to the Oculus Home Tray enabling fluid transition from platform to platform. After Installing Oculus Home , we need to enable third party applications by activating the unknown sources property.

  1. Open Oculus Home client
  2. Log in using personal account information
  3. Using the navigation bar on the left, select settings.
  4. Move to the General tab located at the top of the page
  5. At the top of the list is Unknown Sources, click the sliding button to the right on the same line. The slider will turn blue indicating it is active.

From here we need to ensure that Oculus home is operating on the most up to date software by enabling beta usage. This is a requirement due to the rapid progress PLEXSYS engineers make in rolling out support and capabilities of our XYZ systems.

  1. From Settings(located in the left hand navigation window) select the Beta tab at the top of the page.
  2. locate the Public Test Channel option and click on the slider button to the right , on the same line. The slider will turn blue indicating it is active.

Congratulations, Oculus Home is now prepared to connect with our XYZ platform! You are moments away from activating your enhanced training experience. Please continue on to Steam VR installation and setup.
