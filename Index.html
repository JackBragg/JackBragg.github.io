Oculus Quest/XYZ force multiplier

XYZ is a powerful command and control versatile platform that enables immersive command views of our most popular products. The Virtual Reality headset, Oculus Quest can be paired with our XYZ technology to create a force multiplier either in the field as a standalone system, or enhanced with a dedicated command, control, and communication suite(C3).

This guide will walk you through the process for connecting and configuring your device.

XYZ software works by connecting two separate platforms into a single cohesive system. By utilizing a fully immersive experience, Command staff are augmented with increased situational awareness while reducing environmental distractions.

As of update 12.34, XYZ is now on the Steam marketplace and can be configured by the Steam Client itself while pairing with Oculus Home beta software.

Frequently Asked Questions

"I have a USB type-C to type-A, will this still work?"

As long as the cable is a USB 3.0 then the cable will still work. The USB type-C connection is required only on the Oculus Quest due to that being the only connection type available. The other end of the cable is determined by the computer it is being plugged into.

 "The Oculus Home connection screen is only showing one green light, is XYZ software working?"

The single green light means that the connection between the cable and the headset is not fully connected or secure. Unplugging and restarting the device and software then reconnecting should create a solid connection. If the problem persists, inspect the connection sites to ensure they are free of debris and the latches/ leads are not bent or obstructed.

 "XYZ software is not working, I can't get to the home screen. I can only see the connection screen."

Make sure that the Steam Client is open and the "VR" green box is lit up in the upper right hand corner. If not, please follow the guide to reconnect Oculus Home to Steam VR.
